CHRIS
PINNER

Founder & Head Coach

Chris founded Pinnacle Personal Training in 2010 with a simple idea: that everyone deserves access to expert, personalised coaching in a welcoming environment. Fourteen years later, that idea has grown into Bewdley Bootcamp — the town's only dedicated functional fitness gym.

Having worked with hundreds of clients across all ages, abilities, and goals, Chris brings a depth of experience that's hard to find outside of a major city. Whether you're a complete beginner nervous about walking through the door, or a seasoned athlete looking to push through a plateau, he's likely helped someone in exactly your position.

Outside the gym, Chris is a familiar face in Bewdley — you'll often find him in the Wyre Forest on an early morning run, or watching his kids play sport on a Saturday.

His coaching philosophy is direct and honest: results come from consistent, progressive effort. Not miracle programmes. Not fad diets. Just good movement, good habits, and good support.
