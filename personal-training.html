PERSONAL
TRAINING

Individual attention, a programme built around your goals, and a coach who's invested in your progress. Available as an add-on to classes or as a standalone service.

Personal training at Bewdley Bootcamp begins with a free one-hour consultation. No poke-and-prod assessment — just an honest conversation about what you want to achieve, why it matters to you, and how we'll get there together.

From there, your programme is built around you. Your schedule, your limitations, your goals. Whether that's losing weight, building strength, recovering from injury, improving performance in a sport, or simply feeling better in your body — we've helped people with all of it.

Chris has over 14 years of coaching experience. He's also an expert in making the process feel less daunting than you might expect.
