Class type 01

STRENGTH
TRAINING

Our strength programme is built on compound movements — squats, deadlifts, presses, pulls — designed to build real, functional strength you can use in everyday life and sport.

Sessions are progressive, meaning you'll be challenged at your own level and tracked over time. Whether you're brand new to lifting or looking to push past a plateau, the programme meets you where you are.

Equipment includes barbells, kettlebells, dumbbells, rigs, and battle ropes. No machines. Just honest work.

Class type 02

CARDIO
CONDITIONING

High intensity circuits that push your cardiovascular system and build serious work capacity. Expect AMRAPs, EMOMs, intervals, and timed challenges that are never the same twice.

These sessions are tough — but scalable. Our coaches ensure you're working at your threshold, not someone else's. The goal is consistent improvement, not punishment.

Perfect alongside the strength programme to build a complete functional fitness base.

Class type 03

MOBILITY
TRAINING

Strength and cardio are only part of the picture. Our mobility sessions focus on movement quality — improving range of motion, joint health, and your ability to move freely through life.

Drawing on movement principles from yoga, physiotherapy, and athletic conditioning, these sessions will reduce injury risk, improve posture, and help you recover faster between harder sessions.

A weekly mobility session is non-negotiable if you want to train hard for the long term.
